<ol>
 	<li>Log into your cPanel:
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/login.png" alt="" width="477" height="320" /></li>
 	<li>Navigate to <strong>Files</strong> &gt; <strong>All Applications</strong>:
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-232" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/4.4-all-applications.gif" alt="" width="816" height="304" /></li>
 	<li>Type <strong>Grav</strong> in the top right search bar and press <strong>Enter</strong>. Click <strong>Install this Application</strong>.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-274" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Screen-Shot-2018-04-04-at-11.07.56-AM.png" alt="" width="1435" height="622" /></li>
 	<li>Choose where you would like the domain to live. If you need further assistance on this section, read through our guide on <a href="https://stateu.org/docs/uncategorized/subdomains-vs-subdirectories/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Subdomains Vs. Subdirectories</a>. You'll also want to choose the latest software version, accept the license agreement, and choose your update preferences.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-275" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2018-04-04-at-11.08-AM.png" alt="" width="1382" height="679" /></li>
 	<li>Scroll down. Choose your Backup preferences. In the <strong>Settings</strong> section, name your Grav install, type in your contact details and create a username &amp; password. Make sure that you've copied and pasted these credentials elsewhere, as you'll need them later.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-276" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Screen-Shot-2018-04-04-at-11.10.19-AM.png" alt="" width="1361" height="523" /></li>
 	<li>Scroll down. Here you'll have the option to install an empty version of the tool (i.e. a blank slate) or you can install a predefined package with themes/settings already customized for you. You'll still be able to change settings in a predefined package, but it at least gives you a starting point. When you're ready, click <strong>Install</strong>.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-277" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2018-04-04-at-11.10-AM.png" alt="" width="1367" height="344" /></li>
 	<li>You'll now be redirected to a window where Grav will install.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-278" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2018-04-04-at-11.29-AM.png" alt="" width="1201" height="469" /></li>
 	<li>Once the install has completed, click on the middle admin link to log into your new Grav instance:
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-279" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2018-04-04-at-11.30-AM.png" alt="" width="769" height="238" /></li>
 	<li>Type the credentials that you set during the install process, and click <strong>Login</strong>.
<img class="size-full wp-image-280 aligncenter" src="https://wpdocs.timowens.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Screen-Shot-2018-04-04-at-11.32.00-AM.png" alt="" width="489" height="636" /></li>
</ol>
